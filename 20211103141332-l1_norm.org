#+title: L1 Norm
#+filetags: :math:

The sum of the magnitudes of the vectors in a space.

For example, the L1 norm(distance) of vectors ~(0, 0)~ and ~(3, 4)~, also
the vector $X = (3, 4)$ is
\begin{equation*}
||X||_1 = |3| + |4| = 7
\end{equation*}
