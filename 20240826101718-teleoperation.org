#+title: Teleoperation


1. Hand poses can be captured well from the camera. There is no particular reason to use gloves if not considering haptics.

2. Just a few small motors on fingers are enough for haptics.

3. Immersive active visual feedback is important for more complex, dexterous tasks.

4. For most tasks, _direct joint mapping_ gets around IK and can help you do tasks with high speed and highly dynamic.
