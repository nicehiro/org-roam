#+title: EgoScale: Scaling Dexterous Manipulation with Diverse Egocentric Human Data

The training of EgoScale:

1. pre-training with 20854 hours in-the-wild egocentric recordings spanning diverse real-world environments, incorporating with 829 hours EgoDex dataset that covers 194 tasks. No freezing modules.

2. mid-training with 344 teletop manipulation tasks, with each task captured in approximately 30 human trajectories and 5 robot trajectories, totaling about 50 hours of human data and only 4 hours of robot data. Freezing VLM backbone and only updating vision encoder and DiT head.

3. post-training on task specific robot demonstrations.


The model architecture is nothing new here.
