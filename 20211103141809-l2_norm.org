#+title: L2 Norm
#+filetags: :math:

The shortest distance to go from one point to another.

For example, the L2 norm of the vector $X = (3, 4)$ is
\begin{equation*}
||X||_2 = \sqrt{|3|^2 + |4|^2} = 5
\end{equation*}
