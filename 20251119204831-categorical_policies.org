#+title: Categorical Policies
#+filetags: :discrete:rl:

A categorical policy is like a classifier over discrete actions.

The input is the observation, followed by some number of layers, and then have one final linear layer that gives logits for each action, followed by a [[id:17838CD8-5F69-4433-9BAA-F4E1DA02B071][Softmax]] to convert the logits into probabilities.
