#+title: FP16

FP16 is 16-bit floating point formats, with IEEE 754 half precision:

- 1 sign bit, 5 exponent bits, 10 mantissa bits
- Higher precison, smaller range
- Range: ~6e-8 to 65504
- More prone to overflow/underflow in deep learning
