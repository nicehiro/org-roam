#+title: Long-horizon Robotic Dataset


- *Calvin*: CALVIN: A Benchmark for Language-Conditioned Policy Learning for Long-Horizon Robot Manipulation Tasks. PyBullet & Franka. 2022. [[[https://arxiv.org/pdf/2112.03227.pdf][Paper]]] [[[http://calvin.cs.uni-freiburg.de][Website]]] [[[https://github.com/mees/calvin][Code]]]

#+begin_quote
The dataset itself contains short (average length is around 60 steps, and one language instruction) trajectories, while the evaluation strategy contains long-horizon multi-task settings, with multiple instruction involved in one task.
#+end_quote
